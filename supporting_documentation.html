About the Boston Region MPO's Safe Streets for All Grant Application

Data Sources

Fatality data: 2016-2020 Fatality Analysis Reporting System (FARS): www.nhtsa.gov/file-downloads?p=nhtsa/downloads/FARS/

Population data: 2015-2019 American Community Survey: (Table B01001_001: Total Population)

Classification source: Historically Disadvantaged Community (HDC) Status Tool: datahub.transportation.gov/stories/s/tsyd-k6ij

Regional Datasets

The NOFO instructed applicants to use 1) FARS-based fatality data 2) 2015-2019 American Community Survey-based population, and 3) underserved communities as defined by the USDOT's Justice40 Initiative and the definition of Disadvantaged Communities. MassDOT maintains a robust database of crashes in their IMPACT portal. The Boston Region MPO also maintains a locally-relevant definitions of Environmental Justice communities throught its Title VI work.

A review of these data sources is found here.
